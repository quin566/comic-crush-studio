Refund Policy.

The short version: you do not pay until after you approve your proof, so you are protected from the start. Here is how it works in full.

Last updated: April 2026

How the Process Protects You

Comic Crush Studio is built around a simple premise: you approve the comic before you pay for it. Your proof arrives by email within 48 hours, you request any changes you want at no extra cost, and payment is only collected once you are genuinely happy with the result. This means the risk sits with me, not with you.

At a Glance

Before work starts

Contact me right away and I will cancel the order. No charge, no hassle.

During revisions

Revisions are unlimited. No payment is due yet. We keep working until you are happy.

After files delivered

No refunds once final files are delivered and payment is confirmed.

Before Work Starts

If you need to cancel before I have started on your comic, contact me as soon as possible by replying to your confirmation email. If no work has been done yet, the order will be cancelled with no charge.

During the Revision Stage

If you receive your proof and are not happy with it, do not pay. Just reply and tell me what to change. Revisions are completely free and there is no limit to how many you can request. I will keep revising until the comic is right.

If after multiple rounds of revisions you feel the comic is not working and you want to cancel, contact me directly. Because no payment has been collected at this stage, there is nothing to refund. I will close the order at your request.

After Files Are Delivered

Once you have approved your proof, paid, and received your final high-resolution files, refunds are not available. The work is complete and the files are in your hands.

If there is a technical issue with the files themselves, such as a corrupted download or a missing page, contact me immediately and I will resend them.

Questions or Concerns

If something feels off at any point, reach out before taking any other action. Most issues are straightforward to resolve. Reply to your order email or use the form below.
