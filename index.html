✦ FAQ
How similar does the character look to the real person? +
Very similar. We use AI generation guided by your actual photo — our team crafts and refines the prompts to match your hairstyle, face shape, skin tone, and personality. The more reference photos you provide, the more accurate the result.
How long does it actually take? +
Standard turnaround is 48 hours for most orders. Larger custom projects get a specific timeline after your brief. Rush options are available — just mention it when you order.
What format do I get the comic in? +
High-resolution PDF and PNG files, optimized for both digital sharing and professional printing. Print-ready files are included in all orders of 3 pages or more.
Can I request changes? +
Absolutely. All orders include unlimited revisions until you're completely happy. We won't close out an order until the comic feels exactly right.
What makes a good story idea to submit? +
Anything with a scenario: "my dog escapes every time I open the gate," "we tried to cook a romantic dinner and almost burned the kitchen." Real moments make the best comics. The funnier and more specific, the better.
Can I order as a surprise gift without telling the person? +
Yes — one of our favourite use cases. Just flag it as a surprise and we'll keep all correspondence to your email only. The reaction when they open it is always worth it.
