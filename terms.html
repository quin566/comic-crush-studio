Terms of Service.

The straightforward version of how this works and what you are agreeing to when you place an order.

Last updated: April 2026

What You Are Agreeing To

By placing an order with Comic Crush Studio, you agree to these terms. Comic Crush Studio is operated by Quinten Sinclair as a solo creative service. These terms cover how orders work, what you receive, and what both sides can expect from the process.

The Service

Comic Crush Studio creates custom digital comics based on photos and story details you provide. Each comic is made to order. The service includes:

  • A comic built from your submitted story brief and photos
  • A proof delivered by email within 48 hours
  • Unlimited revisions until you are satisfied
  • Final high-resolution files in PNG and PDF formats

Orders and Payment

Submitting an order form begins the process but does not constitute a binding purchase. Work starts after I receive your photos and confirm the brief.

Payment is collected after you have reviewed and approved your proof. A secure payment link is sent once you confirm you are happy with the comic. Your final files are delivered immediately after payment is confirmed.

Prices are listed on the home page. No hidden fees. The price shown for your package is the price you pay.

Revisions

Revisions are unlimited during the proof stage. If you want changes to any panel, just reply to the proof email and describe what to adjust. I will update and resend.

Revision requests after files have been delivered and payment has been made are handled on a case-by-case basis. If something was clearly wrong on my end, I will fix it. Contact me and we will work it out.

File Delivery and Ownership

Your files are delivered digitally by email. No physical products are shipped.

Once delivered and paid for, the files are yours to keep, print, share, and use for personal purposes. You may not sell, license, or commercially redistribute the files without written permission.

Comic Crush Studio retains the right to display completed comics as portfolio examples, unless you request otherwise at the time of ordering.

Refunds

Refunds are not issued after your final files have been delivered. Because payment is only collected after you approve the proof, you have full opportunity to request changes before committing. See the Refund Policy for more detail.

Orders We May Decline

Comic Crush Studio reserves the right to decline any order at its discretion. This includes but is not limited to requests that involve harmful, harassing, explicit, or defamatory content. If an order is declined after submission, you will not be charged and will be notified by email.

Changes to These Terms

These terms may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when they were last revised. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Questions

If you have questions about these terms, use the form below or reply to any order email and I will get back to you.
