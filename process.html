✦ How It Works

How your comic gets made.

No guessing, no confusion. Here is exactly what happens from the moment you hit submit to the moment your files land in your inbox.

✦ The Full Process

Five steps. 48 hours.

01
Fill Out the Form

Takes about 3 minutes. You tell me who is in the comic, what the story is, and what tone you are going for. Funny? Sweet? Dramatic? Completely unhinged? The more specific you are, the better the final comic. You do not need the whole thing mapped out. Just give me the gist and I will handle the creative direction from there.

About 3 minutes
02
Confirmation and Photos

Right after you submit, a confirmation email hits your inbox. Reply to that email with your photos. Clear face shots work best. The more detail in the photo, the closer the character likeness. You do not need professional shots. A sharp phone photo in decent lighting is completely fine. You can send multiple references and I will pull the best elements from each.

Arrives in minutes
03
Your Comic Gets Built

I read your order, write a detailed creative brief, and take it through the AI pipeline panel by panel. Every panel gets reviewed and refined before it reaches you. If a character's face is off, I fix it. If an expression does not land, I adjust the prompt and generate again. The technology handles the rendering. I handle making it look right.

Done behind the scenes
04
Your Proof Arrives

Within 48 hours, your first look arrives by email. Go through every panel carefully. If anything needs adjusting, just reply and tell me what to change. Revisions are unlimited. I do not close your order until you are genuinely happy with what you see.

Within 48 hours
05
Approve, Pay, and Receive

Once you love it, I send a secure payment link. After payment is confirmed, the full high-res files come straight to your inbox. PNG and PDF, ready to print, frame, post, or gift. That is it. Done.

Files delivered immediately after payment
✦ Good to Know

A few things worth mentioning.

📸
Photos Matter

The better the photo, the closer the character likeness. Front-facing, clear lighting, and a shot where the face is fully visible give the best results. Sunglasses and heavy filters make the job harder. When in doubt, send a few options and I will pick the best one.

✏️
Revisions Are Free

Every order includes unlimited revisions. If you want a different expression, a panel redrawn, or the story adjusted, just say so. I work on your order until it is something you actually love, not just something that was technically delivered.

💳
Pay After Approval

You do not pay anything upfront. Payment is collected only after you have reviewed your proof and are happy with it. You get to see exactly what you are buying before any money changes hands.
