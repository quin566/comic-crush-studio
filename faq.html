✦ Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you want to know before you order. If your question is not here, scroll to the bottom and ask directly.

Your Order
How do I place an order? +
Fill out the order form on the Create page. It takes about 3 minutes. You tell me who is in the comic, what the story is, and what tone you want. Right after you submit, a confirmation email arrives. Reply to that email with your photos and we are off.
How long does it take? +
Standard turnaround is 48 hours or less for most orders. Larger custom projects may take a little longer and I will let you know upfront if that is the case. Rush options are available too. Just mention it in the form or your confirmation email reply.
Can I order as a surprise gift without telling the person? +
Yes, this is one of the best use cases. Flag it as a surprise in your order and I will keep all correspondence to your email only. The reaction when someone opens a comic of themselves is always worth it.
What makes a good story idea? +
Anything with a real scenario. "My dog escapes the yard every single time I open the gate." "We tried to cook a romantic dinner and almost burned the kitchen down." "My dad thought he was fixing the car and made everything worse." Real moments with a clear sequence of events make the best comics. The more specific and funny the detail, the better.
Do I need to have the story fully planned out? +
Not at all. A rough idea is enough to get started. Give me the characters, the general situation, and the feeling you want, and I will build the story arc from there. Most people are surprised by how much comes out of a single sentence of inspiration.
Can I order more than one comic at once? +
Yes. Just submit a separate form for each one or mention it in your order notes and I will sort out the details with you. Bulk gift orders are also welcome.
The Comic Itself
How close will the characters look to us? +
Very close. I use your actual photo as the reference and build the character from there, matching face shape, hairstyle, skin tone, and overall vibe. The more clear and detailed your reference photo, the more accurate the result. Sending multiple reference shots from different angles helps a lot.
Can I request a specific art style? +
Yes. The order form lets you pick from available styles, and you can describe specific preferences in the notes. If you have a reference image or a particular look in mind, mention it and I will do my best to match it.
How many characters can be in the comic? +
It depends on the package. The Single Page and Comic Strip tiers are built for 1 to 2 characters. The 3-Page Comic handles up to 3. The 6-Page handles up to 4. The Full Custom package can include as many as the story calls for. Pets count as characters and are always welcome.
What if I want changes to my proof? +
Just reply to the proof email and tell me what to adjust. Revisions are unlimited. I work on your order until every panel looks right and you are genuinely happy with it. I do not consider an order done until you say it is.
Can you make comics with kids in them? +
Yes. Family comics featuring kids are some of the most popular orders. Just include photos in your submission and note their ages and any personality details that would make the character more accurate.
Photos and Files
What photos work best? +
Clear, front-facing shots in decent lighting. The face should be fully visible with no heavy filters, sunglasses, or obstructions. You do not need professional photos. A sharp phone photo works perfectly. Sending two or three references from slightly different angles gives the best character accuracy.
What file formats do I receive? +
Your final comic is delivered as high-resolution PNG and PDF files. Both are print-ready. PNG works great for sharing digitally or uploading to a print service. PDF is ideal for home printing or sending to a professional printer.
Can I print my comic? +
Yes. All files are delivered at print resolution. You can print at home, upload to any online print service, or take them to a local print shop. Many customers frame their comics or have them printed as poster-size pieces.
Do you offer physical print delivery? +
Not currently. All orders are delivered as digital files. You own the files and can print them anywhere you like, at any size.
Is my order private? +
Yes. Your photos and story details are only used to create your comic. They are not shared publicly, sold, or used for any other purpose. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
Payment and Refunds
How much does a comic cost? +
Prices start at $15 for a single-page comic and go up based on size and complexity. The most popular option is the 3-Page Comic at $39. Full pricing is on the home page under the Pricing section.
When do I pay? +
After you have approved your proof. You do not pay anything upfront. Once you have reviewed the comic and are happy with it, I send a secure payment link. Payment is collected then and your final files follow immediately.
Do you offer refunds? +
Refunds are not available after your files have been delivered. If you have not yet received your proof, contact me before work begins and I will sort it out. During the revision process, I work until you are happy before payment is ever collected, so there is very little risk on your end. See the Refund Policy for full details.
