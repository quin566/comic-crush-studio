Privacy Policy.

Short version: your information is used to make your comic and nothing else. Here is the full version.

Last updated: April 2026

What This Covers

This policy explains what information Comic Crush Studio collects when you place an order, how that information is used, and what is never done with it. Comic Crush Studio is a solo operation run by Quinten Sinclair. There is no legal entity, no marketing team, and no database beyond what is needed to fulfill your order.

What I Collect

When you submit an order form, I collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • The story details and preferences you share in the form
  • The photos you send in reply to the confirmation email

That is it. No accounts, no tracking pixels, no cookies beyond standard site functionality.

How I Use It

Your information is used for one purpose: to create and deliver your comic. Specifically:

  • Your name and email are used to communicate with you about your order
  • Your story details and preferences are used to build the creative brief for your comic
  • Your photos are used as reference material to generate your character likenesses

I do not use your information for marketing, retargeting, or any purpose unrelated to your order.

What I Do Not Do

  • I do not sell your information to anyone
  • I do not share it with third parties except the payment processor used to collect payment after you approve your proof
  • I do not use your photos in any marketing material without your explicit written permission
  • I do not add you to any mailing list without your consent

Your Photos

Photos you send are used only to build your comic. Once your order is complete and files have been delivered, I delete your photos from my working files. I do not store them long-term or use them for any other purpose.

If you would like your photos removed at any point during or after the order process, just send an email and I will take care of it immediately.

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy or how your information is handled, use the form below or reply to any order email.
