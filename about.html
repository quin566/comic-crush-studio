✦ How It Actually Works

Behind the scenes.

01
You Submit

You fill out the order form with your photos, story idea, tone, and preferences. Takes about 3 minutes. I read every word.

02
We Brief

I review your order and build a detailed creative brief: character descriptions, story beats, visual moments, panel layout, and the exact prompts I'll need to bring it all to life.

03
We Generate & Refine

We run your brief through our AI pipeline, then review every single panel — adjusting prompts, editing outputs, and refining until the characters, expressions, and story beats all hit exactly right.

04
You Receive

Your proof arrives by email within 48 hours. I revise as needed, then send the final high-res files. Done.
